# BingoDay Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 2026

BingoDay respects your privacy.

## Information We Collect

BingoDay is designed to store your task data locally on your device.

If you choose to use voice input, BingoDay requests access to:
- Microphone
- Speech Recognition

These permissions are only used to convert your speech into task text inside the app.

## How We Use Information

BingoDay uses information only to provide core app functionality, including:
- Creating and editing tasks
- Marking tasks as completed
- Supporting voice input for task entry

## Data Storage

Task data is stored locally on your device.

## Data Sharing

BingoDay does not sell your personal data.

BingoDay does not share your data with third parties for advertising purposes.

## Your Choices

You can:
- Edit or delete tasks at any time in the app
- Deny microphone or speech recognition permissions in your device settings
